# Urad Dal (Mummy Recipe)

Urad dal cooked and finished with ginger-chilli paste, yogurt, oil, and a ghee-and-clove seasoning.

## Ingredients

- Udad dal (urad dal) (quantity not specified)
- Ginger-chilli paste
- Dahi (yogurt)
- Oil
- Ghee (for seasoning)
- Clove (for seasoning)

## Instructions

1. Boil the dal.
2. Add ginger-chilli paste and dahi.
3. Add oil and a seasoning (tadka) of ghee and clove.
