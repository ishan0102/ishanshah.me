# Toasted Sandwich (Potato-Tomato Masala Filling, Partial)

A brief, partial note describing a spiced potato mixture with tomatoes used for a toasted sandwich filling.

## Ingredients

- salt
- red chilli powder
- dhania/jeera (coriander-cumin seasoning)
- sugar
- potatoes (added to seasoning)
- tomatoes (added after potatoes are done)
- masala (unspecified)

## Instructions

1. Prepare a seasoning with salt, red chilli powder, dhania/jeera, and sugar.
2. Add potatoes to the seasoning and cook until done.
3. Add tomatoes and masala, then turn off the heat.
