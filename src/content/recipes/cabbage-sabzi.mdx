# Cabbage Sabzi

Simple cabbage and capsicum stir-fry tempered with mustard oil and hing, seasoned with turmeric, salt, and green chillies.

## Ingredients

- Mustard oil
- Hing (asafoetida)
- Cabbage
- Capsicum
- Green chillies (spicy)
- Haldi (turmeric)
- Salt

## Instructions

1. Heat mustard oil and add hing.
2. Add cabbage, capsicum, and spicy green chillies.
3. Add haldi and salt and cook until done.
