# Pani Puri Stuffing (Mixed Filling)

A mixed stuffing for pani puri made with boiled/sprouted legumes, potatoes, and boondi, seasoned with chaat spices.

## Ingredients

- moong (boiled or sprouted)
- potatoes (boiled)
- black channa (boiled)
- boondi (salted)
- salt
- chilli powder
- coriander
- chaat masala

## Instructions

1. Combine boiled moong (or sprouted moong), boiled potatoes, boiled black channa, and salted boondi.
2. Season with salt, chilli powder, coriander, and chaat masala.
