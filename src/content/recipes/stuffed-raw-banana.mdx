# Stuffed Raw Banana

Raw bananas stuffed with a spicy besan-based filling, then sautéed and cooked until tender on a tawa or in a pressure cooker.

## Ingredients

- raw bananas (quantity not specified; used for stuffing)
- 9 green chilies (for filling)
- chana dal flour / gram flour (besan) (for filling)
- coriander powder (for filling)
- black salt (for filling)
- turmeric (for filling)
- red chili powder (for filling)
- oil (for filling and cooking)
- garam masala (for filling)
- lemon (for filling)
- tamarind (for filling)
- water (a little)

## Instructions

1. Mix all the filling (masala) ingredients and keep ready.
2. Stuff the raw bananas with the masala.
3. Heat oil; add the stuffed raw bananas and sauté.
4. Add a little water and cover.
5. Cook either on a tawa or in a cooker for 3–4 whistles.

## Tips

- Can be cooked on a tawa or in a pressure cooker (3–4 whistles).
