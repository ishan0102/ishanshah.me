# Tempered Khichdi (Toor Dal & Rice)
*Vaghareli Khichdi*

A simple Gujarati khichdi made with toor dal and rice, finished with a mustard-seed tempering.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup toor dal (pigeon peas)
- 1 cup rice
- salt (to taste)
- turmeric (to taste)
- red chili powder (to taste)
- oil (for tempering (vaghar))
- mustard seeds (rai) (for tempering (vaghar))
- asafoetida (hing) (for tempering (vaghar))

## Instructions

1. Prepare using the same method as Vagharelo Bhaat (as referenced in the source text).
