# Vaghareli Khichdi
*Vaghareli Khichdi*

Rice and tuvar dal khichdi seasoned with turmeric and chili, finished with a mustard-asafoetida tempering, made in the style of vagharelo bhaat.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup tuvar dal (pigeon peas)
- 1 cup rice
- salt
- turmeric
- red chili powder
- oil (for vaghar (tempering))
- mustard seeds (rai) (for vaghar (tempering))
- asafoetida (hing) (for vaghar (tempering))

## Instructions

1. Prepare the khichdi using the same method as Vagharelo Bhaat (mix seasonings, add tempering, then cover and cook briefly on low heat).
