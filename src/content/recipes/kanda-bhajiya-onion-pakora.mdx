# Kanda Bhajiya (Onion Pakora)
*Kanda Bhajiya*

Crisp onion bhajiyas made with a thick chickpea flour batter and fried on medium heat.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups chickpea flour (chana flour)
- 6 pieces onions (sliced)
- red chili powder
- salt
- turmeric
- water (add a little to make a thick batter)
- oil (for frying)

## Instructions

1. Mix spices into the chickpea flour.
2. Add sliced onions and mix well (do not add too much water).
3. Add a little water and make a thick batter.
4. Fry in hot oil on medium flame until done.

## Tips

- Keep the flame on medium while frying.
- Do not add too much water; batter should be thick.
