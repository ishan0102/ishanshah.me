# White Peas (Seasoned)
*White Peas*

White peas soaked overnight, boiled, then seasoned with hing and basic spices.

## Ingredients

- white peas (soak overnight; boil in the morning)
- oil
- hing (asafoetida)
- haldi (turmeric)
- salt
- red chilli (powder implied)
- dania-jeera (coriander-cumin powder)

## Instructions

1. Soak white peas overnight.
2. Boil them in the morning.
3. Heat oil with hing, add the peas, then add haldi, salt, red chilli, and dania-jeera.
