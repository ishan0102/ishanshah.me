# Oadaman (Seasoned Dal Water)
*Oadaman*

A seasoned preparation made by tempering spices and adding them to dal water, then finishing with lemon, sugar, and herbs.

## Ingredients

- oil
- hing (asafoetida)
- rai (mustard seeds)
- jeera (cumin seeds)
- cloves
- cinnamon
- dal water
- salt
- lemon
- sugar
- haldi (turmeric)
- green chillies
- coriander (cilantro)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil and add hing, rai, jeera, cloves, and cinnamon.
2. Add this tempering to the dal water.
3. Add salt, lemon, sugar, haldi, green chillies, and coriander.
