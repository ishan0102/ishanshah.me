# Pani (Rice Flour Flatbread)
*Pani*

A soft rice-flour dough (roti-like) prepared ahead and cooked without adding oil or ghee during frying.

## Ingredients

- rice flour
- green chili
- ginger
- salt
- ghee or oil (listed for dough/mixture)
- dahi (yogurt)
- haldi (turmeric)

## Instructions

1. Mix rice flour with green chili, ginger, salt, dahi, and haldi to make a soft dough like roti (including ghee/oil as indicated in the ingredient list).
2. Make the dough 3–4 hours before cooking.
3. Shape/roll it on a plastic bag.
4. Cook on a hot surface; do not add any oil or ghee while frying.

## Tips

- Make the dough 3–4 hours in advance.
- Shape on a plastic bag.
- Do not add oil or ghee during frying.
