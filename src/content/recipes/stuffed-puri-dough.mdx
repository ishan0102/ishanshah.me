# Stuffed Puri Dough

A maida-wheat flour dough for making stuffed puris, rolled like paratha and deep-fried.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups maida (all-purpose flour)
- 1 cup wheat flour
- ajwain (carom seeds) (a little)
- salt (a little)
- oil (a little)

## Instructions

1. Combine maida, wheat flour, ajwain, salt, and a little oil; make a puri-style dough.
2. Fill with prepared stuffing and roll like an aloo paratha.
3. Deep-fry until done.
4. Serve with aloo sabzi.

## Tips

- Serve/eat with aloo sabzi (as noted).
