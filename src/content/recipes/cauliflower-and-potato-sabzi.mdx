# Cauliflower and Potato Sabzi
*Phoolkobi Batata*

Cauliflower-potato sabzi with peas, mild sweetness, and a mustard-hing tempering, cooked using the same method as cabbage-bataka.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg cauliflower (cut into florets)
- 1 potato
- 2 peas
- garam masala
- red chili powder
- salt (to taste)
- sugar
- oil (for tempering)
- mustard seeds (for tempering)
- asafoetida (hing) (for tempering)

## Instructions

1. Cook using the same method as Cabbage Batata (Kobi Bataka).
