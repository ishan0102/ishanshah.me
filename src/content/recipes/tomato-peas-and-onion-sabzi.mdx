# Tomato, Peas, and Onion Sabzi

A gravy-style mixed sabzi of peas and onions simmered with tomatoes and finished with red chili powder.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg green peas
- 2 onions
- 1/4 kg tomatoes (chopped medium-size)
- salt (to taste)
- turmeric
- coriander powder
- garam masala
- red chili powder (added after second boil)
- oil (for tempering)
- mustard seeds (for tempering)
- cumin seeds (for tempering)
- 2 bowls water

## Instructions

1. Heat oil with mustard seeds and cumin seeds (tempering).
2. Add peas and onions and saut√©.
3. Add all spices except red chili powder and add 2 bowls of water.
4. When it starts boiling, add chopped medium-size tomatoes.
5. When it boils again, add red chili powder and cook.
