# Split/Slit Potato Shaak
*Batakanu Chhatvalnu Shaak*

A simple potato sabzi where spices are added as the potatoes partially cook and the dish is brought to a boil.

## Ingredients

- 6 pieces potato (washed and cut)
- red chilli powder
- turmeric
- coriander powder
- salt (to taste)
- oil (for tempering (vaghar))
- mustard seeds (for tempering (vaghar))
- asafoetida (hing) (for tempering (vaghar))

## Instructions

1. Wash and cut the potatoes.
2. When the potatoes are a little cooked, add turmeric and chilli powder.
3. When it starts to boil, add mustard, coriander powder, and salt.
