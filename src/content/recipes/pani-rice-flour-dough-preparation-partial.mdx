# Pani (Rice Flour Dough Preparation, Partial)
*Pani*

A soft rice-flour dough flavored with green chili, ginger, yogurt, and turmeric; rested for several hours and cooked without oil/ghee (very brief note).

## Ingredients

- rice flour
- green chili
- ginger
- salt
- ghee/oil (listed, but later note says do not use while frying)
- dahi (yogurt)
- haldi (turmeric)

## Instructions

1. Mix rice flour with green chili, ginger, salt, dahi, and haldi to make a soft dough like roti dough.
2. Prepare the dough 3–4 hours before making.
3. Shape/cook it on a plastic bag.
4. Do not use oil or ghee while frying.

## Tips

- Make the dough 3–4 hours in advance.
- Cook on a plastic bag as the base.
