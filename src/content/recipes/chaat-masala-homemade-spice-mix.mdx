# Chaat Masala (Homemade Spice Mix)

A homemade chaat masala blend mixed and stored dry for later use in snacks and chaat.

## Ingredients

- 2 tbsp sanchaal (sandhal) (spelling as in text)
- 3 tbsp salt
- 2 tbsp black pepper powder
- 2 tbsp oil powder (as written)
- 2 tbsp turmeric (haldi)
- 1/2 tbsp asafoetida (hing)
- 4 tbsp amchoor (aamchur)
- 4 tbsp red chilli powder
- 2 tbsp roasted cumin powder (roasted jeera powder)

## Instructions

1. Mix all the ingredients together well.
2. Store in a dry bottle.

## Tips

- Store in a dry bottle.
