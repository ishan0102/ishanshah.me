# Lachko Dal (Loose Tuver Dal)
*Lachko Dal*

Tuver dal boiled with extra water to a loose consistency, then tempered and cooked to thicken without becoming too thick.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup tuver dal
- turmeric (haldi)
- salt
- asafoetida (hing)
- jaggery
- water (boil dal in a little more water)
- tempering (unspecified) (text says 'Then do tempering' without listing ingredients)
- spices (unspecified) (text says 'Then add spices' without listing beyond those above)

## Instructions

1. Boil the tuver dal in a little more water to keep it loose.
2. Do the tempering.
3. Add spices and continue cooking.
4. Let it thicken while cooking; do not make it too thick.
