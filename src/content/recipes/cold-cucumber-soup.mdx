# Cold Cucumber Soup

Chilled blended cucumber-and-yogurt soup flavored with mint and green chilli.

## Ingredients

- 4 Cucumbers
- 1 tsp Green chilli (chopped)
- 2 tsp Mint leaves
- 3 cup Dahi (yogurt)
- Salt (to taste)
- Water (a little, for blending)

## Instructions

1. Chop cucumbers into big pieces.
2. Blend cucumbers with mint leaves, chopped green chillies, a little water, and salt.
3. Add dahi and blend very well.
4. Serve chilled.
