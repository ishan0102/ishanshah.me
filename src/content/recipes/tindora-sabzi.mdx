# Tindora Sabzi
*Tindora*

Ivy gourd (tindora) cooked with potatoes and basic spices, finished with additional masala and optional flavorings like lemon and sugar.

## Ingredients

- Oil
- Hing (asafoetida)
- Potatoes (fried first)
- Tindora (ivy gourd)
- Haldi (turmeric)
- Salt
- Red chilli powder (added after it cooks)
- Dhania-jeera (mentioned as add-in (form/amount not specified))
- Lemon (mentioned as add-in)
- Sugar (mentioned as add-in)
- Chana flour (besan) (mentioned as add-in)
- Garam masala (mentioned as add-in)
- Coriander (mentioned as add-in)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil and add hing.
2. Fry potatoes first.
3. Add tindora, then add haldi and salt.
4. Cook (instruction note: use a plate covered with water).
5. When cooked, add red chilli powder.
6. Optional/mentioned add-ins: dhania-jeera, salt, lemon, sugar, chana flour, garam masala, coriander.

## Tips

- When it cooks, use a plate covered with water (as written).
