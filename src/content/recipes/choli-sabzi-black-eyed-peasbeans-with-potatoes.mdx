# Choli Sabzi (Black-eyed peas/beans with potatoes)
*Choli*

Potatoes and choli cooked with hing, turmeric, and salt, then finished with chilli and coriander-cumin powder and tomatoes.

## Ingredients

- Oil
- Hing (asafoetida)
- Potatoes
- Choli (bean/legume; exact type not specified)
- Haldi (turmeric)
- Salt
- Red chilli powder
- Dhania-jeera
- Tomatoes
- Sugar (little, if needed)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil and add hing.
2. Add potatoes and choli; add haldi and salt.
3. Cook until done.
4. Add red chilli powder and dhania-jeera.
5. Add tomatoes and cook.
6. Add a little sugar if needed.

## Tips

- Add little sugar if needed.
