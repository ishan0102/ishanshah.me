# Moong (Simple Tempered Moong)
*Moong*

Soaked moong cooked with a mustard-hing tempering and basic spices.

## Ingredients

- 4 moong (soaked in water for 1/2 hour)
- red chili powder
- salt (to taste)
- oil (for tempering)
- mustard seeds (for tempering)
- asafoetida (hing) (for tempering)

## Instructions

1. Soak moong in water for 1/2 hour.
2. Heat oil with mustard seeds and hing (tempering).
3. Add moong and add the spices.
4. Cook until done.
