# Mixed Vegetable Sabzi

A simple mixed vegetable preparation cooked with jeera-hing tempering and basic spices, finished with masalas and optional coconut and ginger-chilli-garlic paste.

## Ingredients

- cauliflower (big pieces)
- capsicum (bell pepper)
- potatoes
- onions
- peas
- tomatoes
- oil (proper amount)
- cumin seeds (jeera)
- asafoetida (hing)
- turmeric (haldi)
- salt
- sugar
- red chilli powder
- garam masala
- coriander
- coconut
- ginger-chilli-garlic paste (optional)
- water (placed on lid as described)

## Instructions

1. Cut vegetables into big pieces (as indicated for cauliflower).
2. Heat oil; add cumin seeds and hing.
3. Add all vegetables.
4. Add turmeric, salt, and sugar; cover with a lid with some water on it and cook on slow gas until vegetables are cooked.
5. Add red chilli powder, garam masala, coriander, and coconut.
6. Optionally add ginger-chilli-garlic paste.

## Tips

- Cook covered on low heat with water placed on the lid (as described).
- Ginger-chilli-garlic paste is optional.
