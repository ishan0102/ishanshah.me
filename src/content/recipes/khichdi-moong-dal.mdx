# Khichdi (Moong Dal)
*Khichdi*

A simple moong dal and rice khichdi made using the same approach as vagharelo bhaat, with ghee and cloves as the tempering.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup moong dal
- 1 cup rice
- turmeric
- salt
- asafoetida (hing)
- 1 tsp ghee (for vaghar (tempering))
- cloves (for vaghar (tempering))

## Instructions

1. Prepare using the same method as Vagharelo Bhaat.
