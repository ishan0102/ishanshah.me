# Black Peas (Seasoned)
*Black Peas*

Black peas soaked and boiled, then seasoned similarly to the white peas version.

## Ingredients

- black peas (soak overnight and boil)
- oil
- hing (asafoetida)
- haldi (turmeric)
- salt
- red chilli (powder implied)
- dania-jeera (coriander-cumin powder)

## Instructions

1. Soak black peas overnight and boil them.
2. Heat oil with hing, add the peas, then add haldi, salt, red chilli, and dania-jeera.
