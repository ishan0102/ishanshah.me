# Sukhi Bhaji (Dry Potato Curry)
*Sukhi Bhaji*

A dry-style potato and onion curry cooked covered on low heat with basic Gujarati spices and tempering.

## Ingredients

- 6 potatoes (cut)
- 2 onions
- cumin
- garlic
- red chilli powder
- turmeric
- coriander powder
- salt (to taste)
- oil (for tempering (vaghar))
- mustard (for tempering (vaghar))
- cumin (for tempering (vaghar))

## Instructions

1. Wash and cut the potatoes and onions.
2. Mix and keep covered; cook on low heat.
