# Green Tuvar with Vadli / Surti Papdi Vegetable
*Ringna Vadaar ane Surti Papdi nu Shaak*

A variation of the green tuvar vegetable; instructions indicate it is prepared the same way as the basic tuvar shaak.

## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg tuvar (pigeon peas / tuvar dana) (as per Green Tuvar Bean Vegetable)
- 1 piece onion (as per Green Tuvar Bean Vegetable)
- garam masala (as per Green Tuvar Bean Vegetable)
- cloves (as per Green Tuvar Bean Vegetable)
- black pepper (as per Green Tuvar Bean Vegetable)
- salt (as per Green Tuvar Bean Vegetable)
- turmeric (as per Green Tuvar Bean Vegetable)
- coriander-cumin powder (as per Green Tuvar Bean Vegetable)
- oil (for tempering (vaghar))
- mustard seeds (for tempering (vaghar))
- asafoetida (hing) (for tempering (vaghar))
- 1/2 bowl water (as per Green Tuvar Bean Vegetable)
- vadli / papdi (mentioned as variation; quantity not specified)
- ringna vadaar (mentioned in title; details not specified)

## Instructions

1. Prepare this dish using the same ingredients and method as the Green Tuvar Bean Vegetable.
