# Wheat Siro (Wheat Flour Halwa)
*Wheat Siro*

A wheat-flour version of siro/halwa made by roasting flour in ghee and cooking with sugar, flavored with cardamom and almonds.

## Ingredients

- 1 bowl Wheat flour
- 1 1/2 bowls Ghee (or use water per text (unclear))
- 1 1/2 bowls Water (mentioned as alternative to ghee in text; unclear)
- 1/2 bowl Sugar
- Cardamom
- Almonds

## Instructions

1. Prepare using the same method as semolina siro: roast the flour in ghee, then cook with liquid and sugar, and finish with cardamom and almonds.
