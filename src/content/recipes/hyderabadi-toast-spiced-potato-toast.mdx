# Hyderabadi Toast (Spiced Potato Toast)

Bread toast topped with a spiced mashed potato paste and finished with chutneys, onions, tomatoes, and sev; garlic chutney is optional.

## Ingredients

- potato (boiled; mashed to paste)
- salt
- lemon (use juice)
- sugar
- garam masala
- bread (slices)
- oil (a little; for toasting)
- sweet chutney
- green chutney
- onions (topping)
- tomatoes (topping)
- sev (topping)
- garlic chutney (optional)

## Instructions

1. Make a paste from boiled potato, salt, lemon, sugar, and garam masala.
2. Spread the paste on bread.
3. Toast on a frying pan using a little oil.
4. Top with sweet chutney and green chutney.
5. Add onions, tomatoes, and sev on top.
6. Optionally add garlic chutney.

## Tips

- Garlic chutney can also be added.
