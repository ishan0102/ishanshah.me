# Tadkava (Tempering Buttermilk Drink/Gravy)
*Tadkava*

A tempered buttermilk preparation flavored with pepper and hing, brought to a boil and served hot.

## Ingredients

- 2 pinches tadkava (ingredient as written) (exact ingredient unclear in translation)
- 2 tsp oil
- 1 tsp black pepper
- asafoetida (hing)
- salt
- water (added as needed)
- 1/2 bowl buttermilk

## Instructions

1. Heat oil; add tadkava, black pepper, and hing.
2. When it starts to fry, add water.
3. Add 1/2 bowl buttermilk and more water.
4. Bring to a boil; add salt and hing and serve.
