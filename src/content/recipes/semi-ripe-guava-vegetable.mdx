# Semi-Ripe Guava Vegetable

Guava and potato curry cooked in water with spices and peanuts, with sugar added only after cooling.

## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg guava (semi-ripe; cut into pieces)
- 4 potatoes
- 4 cups water
- salt (to taste)
- green chilies
- ground peanuts
- sugar (add after it cools)
- oil

## Instructions

1. Cook guava pieces and potatoes in 4 cups water.
2. Add oil and all spices except sugar.
3. Do not cover while cooking.
4. Add sugar after it cools.

## Tips

- Do not cover while cooking.
- Add sugar only after the dish cools.
