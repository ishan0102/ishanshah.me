# Bhindi (Okra Stir-Fry, Partial)

Okra cooked with basic tempering and finished with amchur and spice powders; instructions are brief/partial.

## Ingredients

- bhindi (okra)
- aamchur
- red chilli (powder unspecified)
- salt
- haldi (turmeric)
- garam masala
- jeera (cumin)

## Instructions

1. Prepare seasoning (including jeera).
2. Add bhindi and cook until done.
3. Add spices/masala (aamchur, red chilli, salt, haldi, garam masala) and mix.
4. Cover and cook for some time.
