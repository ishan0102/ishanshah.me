# Green Banana (Raw Banana) Vegetable

A savory curry of raw bananas and onions cooked with spices and finished with tamarind for tang.

## Ingredients

- 3 raw bananas (medium)
- 3 onions
- black mustard seeds
- turmeric
- salt (to taste)
- coriander powder
- garam masala
- tamarind (add after cooking)
- oil (for tempering)
- curry leaves (for tempering)
- asafoetida (hing) (for tempering)
- water (a little)

## Instructions

1. Heat the tempering.
2. Add onions and raw bananas and saut√©.
3. Add the masalas.
4. Add a little water and cover.
5. Once cooked, add tamarind.
