# Makki na Chevda (Cornflakes Chivda)
*Makki na Chevda*

Fried cornflakes snack mix tempered with mustard seeds and mixed with seasonings; ingredient quantities vary by number of packets used.

## Ingredients

- 2 packets cornflakes (base case as written (alternative quantities given for 4 packets))
- 5â€“6 pieces chili/pepper (as written)
- salt
- 2 cups milk (for the 2-packet version as written)
- 4 packets cornflakes (alternative version)
- 1 cup milk (alternative version for 4 packets as written)
- sour (khat) (as written; unclear ingredient)
- 1 piece lemon (alternative version)
- coriander (a little; alternative version)
- oil
- mustard seeds (rai)

## Instructions

1. Fry the cornflakes.
2. Heat oil in a pan; add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
3. Add the remaining listed ingredients and mix well.

## Tips

- Quantities vary depending on whether using 2 packets or 4 packets of cornflakes (as written).
