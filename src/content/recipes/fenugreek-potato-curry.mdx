# Fenugreek Potato Curry
*Methi (Fenugreek) (Potato curry)*

Potato curry cooked with methi (fenugreek leaves), spices, and finished with curd for a tangy, creamy taste.

## Ingredients

- 9 potatoes (washed and cut into small pieces)
- fenugreek leaves (methi) (a little; chopped and washed)
- 3 onions
- 1 tsp red chilli powder
- salt (to taste)
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1/2 bowl curd (yogurt)
- 1/2 bowl water
- oil (for tempering (vaghar))
- cumin (for tempering (vaghar))
- mustard seeds (for tempering (vaghar))

## Instructions

1. Wash and cut the potatoes into small pieces.
2. Chop the methi and wash it.
3. Add spices and 1/2 bowl water; bring to a boil.
4. Add the curd and mix.
