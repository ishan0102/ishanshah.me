# Fried Dal (Mung Dal)

Simple sautéed mung dal fried with onion in oil and ghee.

## Ingredients

- mung dal (soaked (text also says cooked/soaked dal when frying))
- 1/2 onion
- oil (for tempering/frying)
- ghee (for tempering/frying)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil and ghee.
2. Add the onion and sauté until light brown.
3. Add the cooked/soaked mung dal and fry.
