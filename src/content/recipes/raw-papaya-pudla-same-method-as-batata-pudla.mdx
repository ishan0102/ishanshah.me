# Raw Papaya Pudla (same method as Batata Pudla)
*Kaccha Papaya*

A spiced raw papaya preparation cooked using the same method as Batata Pudla with mustard-hing tempering.

## Ingredients

- 1/4 kg raw papaya
- 4–5 onion
- 4–5 green chillies
- cumin seeds
- lemon
- red chilli powder
- salt
- oil (for tempering)
- mustard seeds (for tempering)
- hing (asafoetida) (for tempering)

## Instructions

1. Prepare using the same method as Batata Pudla: temper oil with mustard seeds and hing, add onions and green chillies, add papaya, season with spices and lemon, and cook on low flame until done.
