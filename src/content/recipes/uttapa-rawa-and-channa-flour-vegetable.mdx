# Uttapa (Rawa and Channa Flour, Vegetable)

A savory uttapa-style batter made from rawa and channa flour soaked in sour buttermilk, mixed with grated vegetables and aromatics.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups rawa (semolina)
- 1 large tbsp channa flour (besan)
- sour buttermilk (for soaking batter)
- salt
- cabbage (grated)
- carrot (grated)
- capsicum
- onions
- dudhi (bottle gourd)
- tomatoes
- ginger-chillies (added to batter)
- pepper powder (optional)
- jeera (cumin) powder (optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix 2 cups rawa and 1 large tbsp channa flour.
2. Soak the mixture in sour buttermilk for 1/2 hour.
3. Add salt.
4. Add grated cabbage, carrot, capsicum, onions, dudhi, and tomatoes.
5. Add ginger-chillies.
6. Optionally add pepper and jeera powders.

## Tips

- You can also add pepper & jeera powders.
