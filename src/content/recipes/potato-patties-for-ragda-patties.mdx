# Potato Patties (for Ragda Patties)
*Patties*

Seasoned mashed potato patties formed into rounds, meant to be served with ragda and chutneys.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg Potatoes (boiled and mashed)
- Turmeric (haldi)
- Salt (to taste)
- Asafoetida (hing)

## Instructions

1. Boil the potatoes and mash them.
2. Add turmeric, salt, and hing and mix well.
3. Shape into round patties.
