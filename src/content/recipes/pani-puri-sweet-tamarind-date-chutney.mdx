# Pani Puri - Sweet Tamarind-Date Chutney

Sweet chutney made by pressure-cooking tamarind, dates, and jaggery, then blending and seasoning with spices.

## Ingredients

- 100 gm tamarind (amli)
- 100 gm dates
- 1 big lump jaggery
- 1 glass water
- salt (to taste)
- sandhal (sanchaal)
- red chilli powder
- 1/4 tsp cumin powder (jeera powder)

## Instructions

1. Boil tamarind, dates, and jaggery in a pressure cooker with 1 glass of water.
2. Blend and strain the mixture.
3. Add salt, sandhal, red chilli powder, and 1/4 tsp jeera powder.
