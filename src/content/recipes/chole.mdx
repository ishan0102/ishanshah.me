# Chole

A chickpea curry cooked with onion-tomato base, ginger-garlic paste, and warming spices including bay leaves and garam masala.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups kabuli chana (chickpeas) (soaked overnight)
- 3 onions
- 3 tomatoes
- 4 potatoes
- ginger
- 6–7 cloves garlic
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- cumin
- coriander powder
- turmeric
- salt
- 6–7 bay leaves
- garam masala
- oil
- green chilies (included in paste with ginger/garlic/onion; quantity not specified)
- chickpea flour (besan) (optional; 'if needed' add a little)

## Instructions

1. Cook/boil the soaked chickpeas (chana).
2. Make a paste of ginger, garlic, chilies, and onions.
3. Heat oil and sauté this paste.
4. Add tomato paste.
5. If needed, add a little besan.
6. Add all masalas.
7. Add the boiled chana and cook.

## Tips

- If needed, add a little besan.
