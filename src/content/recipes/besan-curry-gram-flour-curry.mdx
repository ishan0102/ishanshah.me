# Besan Curry (Gram Flour Curry)
*Besan*

A simple curry made by roasting flour in a tempered base and simmering with buttermilk/chaas and spices.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup buttermilk (chaas)
- 2 tsp flour (as written in the source)
- 2â€“3 green chilies
- 3 tbsp ghee
- salt (to taste)
- red chili powder (to taste)
- turmeric (to taste)
- oil (for tempering)
- mustard seeds (for tempering)
- onions (mentioned in method but not listed in ingredients)
- 1/2 cup water

## Instructions

1. Heat oil for tempering and fry the onions and chilies.
2. When the onions turn light red, add the flour and roast it.
3. Add 1/2 cup water.
4. When it comes to a boil, add ghee, then add salt and spices.
