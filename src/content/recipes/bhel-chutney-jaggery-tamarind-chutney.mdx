# Bhel Chutney (Jaggery Tamarind Chutney)

A sweet-tangy chutney made by boiling jaggery with tamarind and seasoning with spices and pani-puri masala, served after cooling.

## Ingredients

- 100 g fresh jaggery
- tamarind
- salt
- chili powder
- 2 tsp pani-puri masala

## Instructions

1. Boil fresh jaggery and tamarind together; when it comes to a boil, simmer.
2. Add salt, chili powder, and pani-puri masala.
3. Cool and serve.
