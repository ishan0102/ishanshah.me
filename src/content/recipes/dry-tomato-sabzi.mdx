# Dry Tomato Sabzi

Quick sautéed tomato sabzi cooked with simple spices and a ghee-cumin tempering.

## Ingredients

- 4–5 tomatoes
- red chili powder
- salt (to taste)
- turmeric
- about 2 tsp mustard seeds
- ghee (for tempering)
- cumin seeds (for tempering)

## Instructions

1. Heat ghee and cumin seeds (tempering).
2. Add tomatoes.
3. Add spices and sauté.
4. When it releases aroma, remove and serve.
