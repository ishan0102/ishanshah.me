# Black Chana (Boiled and Seasoned)
*Chana (black chana)*

Black chickpeas soaked overnight, boiled, and seasoned with spices and a hing tempering.

## Ingredients

- white chana (chickpeas) (soak overnight)
- haldi (turmeric)
- salt
- red chilli powder
- aamchur (dry mango powder)
- black pepper powder
- oil (for tempering)
- hing (asafoetida) (for tempering)
- chole masala (optional)

## Instructions

1. Soak black chana overnight.
2. Boil the chana.
3. Add haldi, salt, and red chilli powder.
4. Add a tempering of hing (in oil).

## Tips

- You can also add chole masala.
