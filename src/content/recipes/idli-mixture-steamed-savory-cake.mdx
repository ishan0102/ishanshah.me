# Idli Mixture (Steamed savory cake)
*Idli Mixture*

A steamed idli-like batter made from rice flour and maida with spices and curd, rested then steamed on medium heat.

## Ingredients

- 3 bowls rice flour
- 1 kg maida (refined flour) (or mixed/joined flour (as written))
- garlic paste
- salt
- turmeric (haldi)
- hing (asafoetida)
- 2 tsp sugar
- 2 tsp curd (yogurt)
- oil (a little, as required; plus for greasing)
- water (as needed for batter)

## Instructions

1. Mix maida and all spices into the flour.
2. Mix in the curd.
3. Add water as needed to make a batter.
4. Cover and rest the batter.
5. Grease a plate and pour in the batter.
6. Steam on medium heat until cooked.
7. Remove and let cool slightly before serving.
