# Poda (Besan-based Snack Batter)
*Poda*

A partially described besan-flour mixture with spices and chopped vegetables that is soaked/rested before use.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup besan (gram flour)
- 1/2 cup wheat flour
- 1 tbsp rice flour
- 2 tbsp dal (type not specified)
- red chilli powder
- salt
- onion
- tomato
- green chillies

## Instructions

1. Combine besan, wheat flour, rice flour, dal, red chilli powder, salt, and chopped onion, tomato, and green chillies.
2. Soak/rest the mixture for about 1/2 hour.
