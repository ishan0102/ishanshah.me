# Lapsi (Cracked Wheat Porridge/Dessert)
*Lapsi*

A cracked-wheat milk dessert cooked in a pressure cooker and enriched with ghee, khoya, and nuts, flavored with cloves and cardamom.

## Ingredients

- 1 bowl cracked wheat (lapsi)
- 2 bowls milk
- 3 pieces cloves
- 3 pieces cardamom
- 2â€“4 pieces almonds
- 1 bowl khoya
- 2 tsp ghee
- water (a little; added with khoya)
- dry fruits (added at the end (unspecified))

## Instructions

1. Mix lapsi with milk and pressure cook for about 3 whistles.
2. Add cloves and cardamom and cook.
3. Add ghee and cook.
4. Add khoya and a little water and cook.
5. Add almonds and dry fruits, then remove from the stove.
