# Bhel (components: chutneys)

Notes for making chutneys commonly used in bhel; sweet chutney is referenced from earlier pages, while green and garlic chutneys are specified here.

## Ingredients

- Sweet chutney (same as above (not provided on these pages))
- Coriander (for green chutney)
- Green chilli (for green chutney)
- Ginger (for green chutney)
- Curry leaves (for green chutney)
- Salt (for green chutney)
- Lemon (for green chutney)
- Sugar (for green chutney)
- Garlic (for garlic (red) chutney)
- Red chilli powder (for garlic (red) chutney)
- Salt (for garlic (red) chutney)
- Jeera powder (for garlic (red) chutney)

## Instructions

1. Green chutney: Grind coriander, green chilli, ginger, curry leaves, salt, lemon, and sugar to a paste.
2. Garlic (red) chutney: Grind garlic, red chilli powder, salt, and jeera powder to form a paste.
