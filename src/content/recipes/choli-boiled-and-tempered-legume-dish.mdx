# Choli (Boiled and Tempered Legume Dish)
*Choli*

Boiled choli cooked with basic spices and finished with a mustard-carom seed tempering.

## Ingredients

- choli (boiled first)
- haldi (turmeric)
- salt
- red chilli (powder not specified)
- dhana-jeera (coriander-cumin powder)
- oil (for tempering)
- rai (mustard seeds) (for tempering)
- ajmo (carom seeds/ajwain) (for tempering)

## Instructions

1. Boil the choli.
2. Add haldi, salt, red chilli, and dhana-jeera.
3. Prepare a tempering with rai and ajmo in oil and add it.
