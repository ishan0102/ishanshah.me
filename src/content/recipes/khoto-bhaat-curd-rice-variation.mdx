# Khoto Bhaat (Curd Rice Variation)
*Khoto Bhaat*

A simple variation of tempered rice made by mixing curd into vagharelo bhaat.

## Ingredients

- Vagharelo Bhaat (tempered rice) (prepared as per recipe)
- 2–3 tbsp curd (yogurt)

## Instructions

1. Prepare vagharelo bhaat.
2. Mix in 2–3 tbsp curd to make khoto bhaat.
