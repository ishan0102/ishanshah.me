# Raw Dhokla (Rawa Dhokla)

A steamed semolina (rawa) dhokla made by soaking rawa in sour buttermilk, then aerating with baking soda and finishing with a mustard-curry leaf-sesame tempering.

## Ingredients

- rawa (semolina)
- sour buttermilk (for soaking)
- ginger
- green chillies
- salt
- oil (heated with water)
- water (heated with oil)
- soda bi-carb (baking soda)
- rai (mustard seeds) (for seasoning/tempering)
- curry leaves (for seasoning/tempering)
- til (sesame seeds) (for seasoning/tempering)
- fresh coriander (cilantro) (garnish)
- fresh coconut (garnish)

## Instructions

1. Soak rawa in sour buttermilk for at least 1/2 hour.
2. Add ginger, green chillies, and salt to the batter.
3. Heat oil and water together; when it starts boiling, add soda bi-carb and immediately add it into the batter.
4. Prepare a seasoning/tempering with rai, curry leaves, and til and add it.
5. Top with fresh coriander and fresh coconut.
