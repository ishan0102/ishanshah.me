# Poda (soaked batter snack) - partial
*Poda*

Partial recipe for a besan-based mixture with wheat and rice flour, dal, and vegetables; instructed to soak/rest before use.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup besan
- 1/2 cup wheat flour
- 1 tbsp rice flour
- 2 tbsp dal (type not specified)
- red chilli powder
- salt
- onion
- tomato
- green chillies

## Instructions

1. Combine the listed ingredients.
2. Soak/rest for about 1/2 hour.
