# Vatana (Spiced Peas with Jaggery and Tamarind)
*Vatana*

Soaked and boiled vatana finished with a mustard-cumin tempering and sweet-sour flavors from jaggery and tamarind.

## Ingredients

- vatana (dried peas) (soak overnight and boil)
- oil
- rai (mustard seeds)
- jeera (cumin seeds)
- haldi (turmeric)
- salt
- red chilli powder
- jaggery
- tamarind

## Instructions

1. Soak vatana overnight and boil them.
2. Heat oil with rai and jeera and add the vatana.
3. Add haldi, salt, red chilli powder, jaggery, and tamarind.
