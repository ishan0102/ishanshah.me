# Onion-Potato Vegetable
*Kanda Batata Nu Shaak*

A simple Gujarati sabzi of onions and potatoes cooked with mustard tempering, ginger, green chilies, and spices.

## Ingredients

- 4–5 pieces potatoes
- 3 pieces onions
- 4 pieces green chilies
- garam masala (listed twice; includes 1 tsp at end)
- potato spices (batana masala)
- cloves
- black pepper
- turmeric (haldi)
- coriander-cumin powder (dhaniya-jeeru)
- 2 tsp chickpea flour (besan)
- 2 tsp oil
- 2 tsp tamarind (use as tamarind water; added last)
- water (add to boil and to make tamarind water)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil; add onions (and other aromatics as written) and sauté.
2. Add potatoes, turmeric, chickpea flour, and potato masala; sauté.
3. Add besan and other spices; sauté.
4. Add water and boil.
5. Add tamarind water at the end and finish the potato vegetable.
