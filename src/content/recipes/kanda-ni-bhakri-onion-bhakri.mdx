# Kanda Ni Bhakri (Onion Bhakri)
*Kanda Ni Bhakri*

Wheat flour flatbread mixed with onions, green chillies, coriander, and extra oil, then fried in ghee.

## Ingredients

- wheat flour
- salt
- green chilies
- coriander
- oil (little more than usual)
- onions
- ghee (for frying)

## Instructions

1. Mix wheat flour with salt, green chilies, coriander, oil, and onions.
2. Rest the mixture; bind/adjust the dough while preparing.
3. Fry the bhakri using ghee.
