# Masoor (Seasoned Boiled Masoor with Yogurt)
*Masoor*

Masoor cooked with onions and spices, finished with a hing tempering and some yogurt.

## Ingredients

- masoor (red lentils) (boiled first)
- onions (added while boiling)
- haldi (turmeric)
- salt
- red chilli powder
- oil (for tempering)
- hing (asafoetida) (for tempering)
- dahi (yogurt) (add at end)

## Instructions

1. Boil the masoor, adding onions while boiling.
2. Add haldi, salt, and red chilli powder.
3. Add a tempering of oil and hing.
4. Finally add some dahi.
