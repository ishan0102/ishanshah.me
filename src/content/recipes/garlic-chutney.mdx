# Garlic Chutney

A quick spicy chutney made by grinding garlic with tomatoes and red chilies, seasoned with salt.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup garlic
- 4 tomatoes
- 8 red chilies
- salt
- water (a little, for grinding)

## Instructions

1. Grind garlic, tomatoes, red chilies, and salt together, adding a little water as needed.
