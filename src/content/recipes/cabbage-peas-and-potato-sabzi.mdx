# Cabbage, Peas, and Potato Sabzi
*Kobi Bataka*

Cabbage cooked with potatoes and boiled green peas, tempered with cumin and hing, lightly sweetened and spiced.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 kg cauliflower
- 1/4 kg potatoes
- 100 gms green peas
- oil
- cumin seeds (jeera)
- asafoetida (hing)
- turmeric (haldi)
- sugar (omit if using ginger-garlic-chilli paste)
- salt
- red chilli powder
- garam masala
- coriander-cumin powder (dhania-jeera) (a little)
- tomatoes (optional; chopped)
- ginger-garlic-chilli paste (optional; added at the end)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil; add cumin seeds and hing.
2. Add potatoes and peas, then add cauliflower.
3. Add turmeric, sugar, and salt; cover and cook on low gas until done.
4. Add red chilli powder, garam masala, and a little dhania-jeera.
5. Optionally add chopped tomatoes.
6. Optionally add ginger-garlic-chilli paste and chopped tomatoes at the end; if adding this paste, do not add sugar.

## Tips

- Chopped tomatoes can be added.
- Ginger-garlic-chilli paste can be added at the end; if using it, omit sugar.
