# Cooked Rice (Direct, with Tempering)

Simple cooked rice prepared in a cooker with spices and a tempering of whole spices.

## Ingredients

- 1 bowl rice (soaked rice mentioned in method)
- ghee
- curd (yogurt)
- masala
- cloves
- oil (for tempering)
- cumin (for tempering)
- black pepper (for tempering)
- cinnamon (for tempering)
- water (amount not specified)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil and make the tempering with cumin, black pepper, and cinnamon.
2. Add the soaked rice and water, and add masala.
3. Cook in a cooker.
