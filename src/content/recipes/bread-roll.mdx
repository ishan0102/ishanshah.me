# Bread Roll

Fried bread rolls stuffed with a spiced potato mixture and herbs.

## Ingredients

- 4 slices bread slices
- potatoes (boiled and mashed)
- seasonings/spices (unspecified)
- onion (finely chopped)
- coriander (chopped)
- 2 pieces green chilies (finely chopped)
- salt
- oil (for frying)
- water (for dipping bread slices)

## Instructions

1. Mash boiled potatoes; add salt and spices and mix.
2. Add finely chopped onion, green chilies, and coriander; mix.
3. Dip bread slices in water and squeeze out excess water.
4. Fill bread with the potato mixture, shape into rolls, and fry until done.
