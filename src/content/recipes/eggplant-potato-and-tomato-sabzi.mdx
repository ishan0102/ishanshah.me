# Eggplant, Potato, and Tomato Sabzi
*Baingan, Batata, Tameta*

A variation of the potato-tomato sabzi where eggplant pieces are added along with potatoes.

## Ingredients

- eggplant (cut into pieces; add with potatoes)
- potatoes
- tomatoes

## Instructions

1. Prepare the dish the same way as the Batata-Bataka-Tameta recipe, adding eggplant pieces along with the potatoes.
