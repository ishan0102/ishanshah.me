# Green Choliya Sabzi
*Green Choliya*

Green choliya cooked with mustard-hing tempering and basic spices, simmered slowly and stirred occasionally.

## Ingredients

- 400 g choliya (chopped)
- red chili powder
- salt (to taste)
- turmeric
- dhana-jeera (coriander-cumin powder)
- peas (mentioned in method; quantity not specified)
- oil (for tempering)
- mustard seeds (for tempering)
- asafoetida (hing) (for tempering)
- water (a little)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil with mustard seeds and hing (tempering).
2. Add chopped choliya.
3. Add peas, then add spices.
4. Cover and cook; add a little water if needed.
5. Keep on medium gas and stir occasionally.
6. Cook for 1/2 hour on low gas.
