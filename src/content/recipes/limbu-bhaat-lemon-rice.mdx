# Limbu Bhaat (Lemon Rice)
*Limbu Bhaat*

Tempered rice flavored with peanuts, turmeric, and lemon juice.

## Ingredients

- rice (cooked rice implied)
- peanuts
- lemon (juice implied)
- turmeric
- mustard seeds (for tempering)
- oil (for tempering)
- curry leaves (for tempering)
- ginger (small piece)
- salt (added after rice)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil; add mustard seeds and curry leaves, then add the ginger.
2. When the tempering is ready, add peanuts.
3. Add the rice.
4. Add salt, turmeric, and lemon.
