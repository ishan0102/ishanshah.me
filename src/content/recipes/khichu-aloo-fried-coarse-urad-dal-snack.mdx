# Khichu (Aloo) - Fried Coarse Urad Dal Snack

Soaked urad dal is coarsely ground with ginger, tempered with spices, and fried until crisp.

## Ingredients

- urad dal (soaked 8–10 hours)
- ginger paste
- oil (for tempering/frying)
- jeera (cumin)
- hing (asafoetida)
- haldi (turmeric)
- salt
- red chilli powder

## Instructions

1. Soak urad dal for 8–10 hours.
2. Discard the remaining soaking water.
3. Grind the dal coarsely and mix in ginger paste.
4. Heat oil; add jeera and hing.
5. Add haldi, salt, and red chilli powder.
6. Add the ground dal mixture and fry until crisp.
