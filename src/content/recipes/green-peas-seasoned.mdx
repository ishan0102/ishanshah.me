# Green Peas (Seasoned)
*Green Pea*

Green peas prepared using the same simple hing-and-spice seasoning method as the other peas.

## Ingredients

- green peas
- oil
- hing (asafoetida)
- haldi (turmeric)
- salt
- red chilli (powder implied)
- dania-jeera (coriander-cumin powder)

## Instructions

1. Prepare green peas using the same method as the white peas: season in oil with hing and add haldi, salt, red chilli, and dania-jeera.
