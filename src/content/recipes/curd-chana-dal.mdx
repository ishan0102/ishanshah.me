# Curd Chana Dal
*Curd Chana Dal*

Chana dal cooked with curd and spices, finished with a curry-leaf and hing tempering for a tangy, spiced dal preparation.

## Ingredients

- 200 g curd (yogurt)
- 3/4 bowl chana dal
- 5â€“6 green chilies
- cumin seeds
- ginger
- turmeric
- red chili powder
- 9 lemons lemon juice (juice)
- jaggery (a little)
- oil (for tempering)
- curry leaves (for tempering)
- asafoetida (hing) (for tempering)
- water (use only if needed (see note))

## Instructions

1. Cook the curd and chana dal together in a cooker.
2. Add the other spices and cook again.
3. Add the tempering, then remove from heat.

## Tips

- Do not add too much water to the curd.
- If the curd is thick and water is needed, add only a little water.
