# Cold Salad (Tomato, Capsicum & Yogurt)

Chilled salad of peeled, de-seeded tomatoes and thinly sliced capsicum mixed with salted thickened yogurt and peanuts (optionally coconut).

## Ingredients

- Tomatoes (boiled briefly, peeled, de-seeded, cut in long slices)
- Capsicum (cut thin)
- Dahi (yogurt) (tied in a muslin cloth for some time (to thicken))
- Salt
- Roasted peanuts
- Grated fresh coconut (optional)
- Coriander (for garnish)

## Instructions

1. Cut capsicum thin.
2. Prepare tomatoes: make a cross mark on the bottom of each tomato, boil water, add tomatoes briefly (do not overboil), remove and put in cold water, then peel.
3. De-seed peeled tomatoes and cut into long slices.
4. Combine tomatoes and capsicum in a bowl.
5. Add salt, roasted peanuts, and thickened (hung) dahi; mix well.
6. Optionally add grated fresh coconut.
7. Garnish with coriander and serve chilled.

## Tips

- Do not keep tomatoes in boiling water for long, otherwise they will get cooked from inside.
- You can also put grated fresh coconut.
