# Besan Sev
*Sev*

Crisp fried sev made by pressing a spiced besan dough/batter through a sev maker into hot oil.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups gram flour (besan)
- salt
- red chilli powder
- hing (asafoetida)
- turmeric powder
- water (as needed to make soft dough/batter)
- oil (for frying)

## Instructions

1. Mix besan with salt, red chilli powder, hing, turmeric, and enough water to make a soft dough/batter.
2. Press the mixture through a sev maker into hot oil.
3. Fry until crisp.
