# Black Peas Masala
*Kala Vatana Masala*

Black peas and onions cooked in a tempered spice base and simmered with water into a masala-style sabzi.

## Ingredients

- 3 black peas (kala vatana)
- 3 onions
- red chili powder
- salt (to taste)
- turmeric
- coriander powder
- oil (for tempering)
- mustard seeds (for tempering)
- cumin seeds (for tempering)
- water (as needed)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil with mustard seeds and cumin seeds (tempering).
2. Add black peas and onions.
3. Add spices and saut√© well.
4. Add water and cook.
