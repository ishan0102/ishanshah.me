# Rajgira Puri (Amaranth Flatbread/Puri, Partial)
*Rajgira Pur*

A rajgira (amaranth) dough prepared with hot water only, without salt or oil (brief note).

## Ingredients

- rajgira flour (amaranth flour) (implied)
- hot water (to make dough)

## Instructions

1. Use hot water to make the dough.
2. Do not add salt or oil.
