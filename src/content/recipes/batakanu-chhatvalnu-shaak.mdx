# Batakanu Chhatvalnu Shaak
*Batakanu Chhatvalnu Shaak*

A potato curry cooked with basic spices and finished by adding tempering/spices as it boils.

## Ingredients

- 6 pieces potato
- red chilli powder
- turmeric
- coriander powder
- salt (to taste)
- oil (for tempering (vaghar))
- mustard (for tempering (vaghar))
- asafoetida (for tempering (vaghar))

## Instructions

1. Wash and cut the potatoes.
2. When the potatoes are a little cooked, add turmeric and chilli powder.
3. When it starts to boil, add mustard, coriander powder, and salt.
